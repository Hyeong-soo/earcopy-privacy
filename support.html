EarCopy Support

EarCopy is a music practice app that lets you slow down music, loop sections, adjust pitch, and use an equalizer to master playing by ear.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import songs?

Tap the + button in the Library tab. You can import audio files from the Files app or browse your Apple Music library.

Why can't I use EQ or pitch with Apple Music?

Due to Apple Music streaming restrictions, EQ and pitch adjustment are only available for local audio files. Apple Music songs support speed adjustment only.

How do I loop a section?

Use the A-B Loop controls below the transport buttons. Set the start (A) and end (B) points, then enable the loop to repeat that section.

What audio formats are supported?

EarCopy supports MP3, AAC, WAV, AIFF, M4A, and other formats supported by iOS.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All your songs, bookmarks, and settings are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers.
